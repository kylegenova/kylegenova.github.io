About Me

I'm a Research Scientist at Google, on Thomas Funkhouser's team within the CCI vision research group led by Bill Freeman and Thomas Funkhouser in the Vision & Robotics Group. Before that, I was an undergraduate in Computer Science at Cornell University, where I worked with David Williamson in the areas of theoretical computer science and combinatorial optimization.
